History of Aprendo International School

Aprendo International School was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide a world-class education that embraces diversity, fosters innovation, and prepares students to become global citizens. The founders, a group of passionate educators and community leaders, saw the need for a school that would bridge cultures and educational systems, offering an inclusive, high-quality curriculum for students from all backgrounds. Starting with a small cohort of 50 students in a rented facility, Apendo has grown steadily and now serves over 1,500 students from more than 40 countries. Over the years, the school has expanded its facilities to include state-of-the-art classrooms, science laboratories, sports complexes, and cultural exchange programs that enrich the educational experience. Apendo is renowned for its focus on holistic development, combining academic excellence with leadership skills, social responsibility, and a deep appreciation for global cultures.

Core Values of Apendo International School

  • Global Citizenship Apendo believes in cultivating students who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible, culturally aware, and ready to contribute to the global community. We encourage students to embrace diversity, understand global challenges, and actively engage in making the world a better place.
  • Academic Excellence We are committed to providing a rigorous academic curriculum that promotes intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. We believe that every student is capable of achieving their personal best and that education should empower students to reach their full potential.
  • Integrity and Respect At Apendo, we foster a culture of honesty, transparency, and mutual respect. We believe that these values are essential for creating a supportive and positive learning environment. Our students are encouraged to act with integrity in their interactions with others, both within and outside of school.
  • Innovation and Creativity We aim to inspire our students to think creatively and embrace innovative solutions to challenges. Apendo encourages out-of-the-box thinking, collaboration, and adaptability, ensuring that students are prepared for an ever-changing world.
  • Holistic Development Apendo values the development of the whole child. We prioritize not only academic growth but also emotional, social, and physical well-being. Our students are provided with opportunities to engage in sports, arts, leadership programs, and community service, ensuring a balanced and fulfilling education.
  • Sustainability We recognize the importance of environmental stewardship and sustainability in today’s world. Apendo encourages students to adopt sustainable practices and become advocates for the environment, both locally and globally.
  • Empathy and Compassion Apendo fosters an environment where students show kindness, empathy, and care for one another. We believe that strong emotional intelligence and a compassionate outlook are key to building lasting relationships and fostering a harmonious school community.
